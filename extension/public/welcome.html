Welcome to Nebula

Nebula is a privacy-first AI copilot that lives inside Chrome. Trigger the command palette anywhere, bundle page context locally, and let Nebula act like a human teammate—only when you give the go-ahead.

Set your hotkey Ctrl / ⌘ + Shift + Space

1. Grant core permissions

Click the puzzle icon → Nebula → Allow the following to unlock the basics:

  • activeTab and tabs for navigation
  • storage to keep preferences
  • commands for the global hotkey

2. Optional boosts

Enable only if you want deeper recall:

  • history & sessions for Spotlight reopen
  • downloads for CSV export
  • clipboard to push AI answers

3. Privacy guardrails

Nebula masks obvious PII, keeps context local, and lets you view exactly what was shared with the AI every time.

4. Try a quick win

Open any page and press your hotkey:

  • “Summarize this page in 3 bullets”
  • “Switch to my email tab”
  • “Fill the job title field with Software Engineer”
Toggle Safe Mode anytime from the sidebar. Nebula never sends chrome:// pages or private browsing data.
